Ingredients

  • 1 cup Butter or margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown sugar, packed
  • 2 Eggs, large
  • 2 1/4 cups Flour, unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon Backing soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 cup Walnuts or Pecans, chopped
  • 2 cups KCC Bittersweet 60% Cacoa Baking Chips

Baking instructions

Heat oven to 375F

Stir flour with backing soda and salt; set aside. In a large mixing bowl, beat butter with sugar, and brown sugar at medium speed until creamy and lightening in color. Add eggs and vanilla, one at a time. Mix on low speed until incorporated.

Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed misture. Stir in nuts and chocalate chips. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes until chocolate chip cookie is ready to eat. Enjoy!!
